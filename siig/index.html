About

The SIIG (Spatial Intelligent and Interaction Group) is a research group under Zhejiang Provincial Key Laboratory of BCCITA and Institute of Cognitive Computing and Intelligent Interaction.

Our primary research areas focus on the intersection of spatial intelligence and embodied intelligence. We approach problems through three guiding principles—unified systems, generalized methods, and robust performance. Key topics include SLAM, localization, reconstruction, representation, interaction, and related fields. An overview of these directions and core results can be found in the figure below.

我们的主要研究方向聚焦空间智能与具身智能的交叉，围绕系统的统一性、通用性和鲁棒性这三个方面开展研究工作。主要研究方向包括 SLAM、定位、重建、表示、交互以及其他相关方向。方向汇总与核心成果可在下图中查看。
